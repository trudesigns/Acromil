Acromil is committed to full compliance with the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) and federal immigration laws. These laws require that all individuals pass an employment verification procedure before they are permitted to work. This procedure requires that every individual complete an I-9 form, which includes submission of valid, original documents that confirm their identity and authorization to work in the United States. Additionally, please note that we utilize the E-verify system to validate any documents presented in support of the I-9 form.

Acromil Corporation is an Equal Opportunity Employer and does not discriminate based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, physical or mental handicap, or military status. Acromil Corporation is a Drug-Free Workplace in compliance with the Drug-Free Workplace Act of 1988. A test for controlled substances may be required as a condition of any offer of employment.

Acromil offers competitive compensation and a generous comprehensive benefit package, including medical insurance with HMO and PPO options, dental with DHMP and DPPO options, vision, short term disability, long term disability, life insurance, 401K, paid vacations, sick time and paid holidays.
