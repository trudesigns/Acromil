ABOUT ACROMIL

COMPANY CULTURE

A company culture is its personality. It’s the company’s mix of the values, ethics, goals, team building, and safe work environment. At Acromil, we strive to ensure that these principles are held in the highest regard, for the benefit of our employees, customers, suppliers and our community. At the core of our culture is the recognition that our employees are our most important resource, and our customers are the foundation for our success. We seek to be a company where our employees, along with our customers and our suppliers know that they are valued and respected. By listening, sharing, keeping an open mind and acknowledging their value, we know that we can draw on each other’s strengths to produce the best possible results for our customers. We regularly bring our employees together to celebrate our successes and openly share with them our goals and vision for the company.

Acromil puts a premium on the education and training of our team. We are committed to helping them achieve their full personal and professional potential with training programs that assist them in developing and growing their work skills. Recognizing the value and importance of providing a clean, healthy working environment, we also work together with and train all of our employees in safe work practices and habits.
